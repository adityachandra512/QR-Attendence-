Transforming Education

Schools in India still rely on traditional bookkeeping methods for maintaining records. This makes the process time-consuming and maintaining these records for a long time becomes difficult.

Our solution digitizes the entire process. Each student receives a unique QR code printed on their uniform. Teachers use a mobile to scan these codes, automatically updating attendance in digital format.
